Why is it necessary to identify cosmetics products?

Safety and Health: Proper identification of cosmetics products helps ensure the safety and health of consumers. By knowing the specific ingredients and components of a product, individuals can make informed choices and avoid potential allergens or substances that may cause adverse reactions.

Quality Assurance: Proper identification is crucial for quality control purposes. It allows manufacturers to track and monitor the production, distribution, and sale of their cosmetics products. Identification systems such as batch numbers or lot codes help in identifying specific batches or production runs, enabling efficient traceability in case of product recalls or quality issues.
